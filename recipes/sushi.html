Sushi

Description

To make sushi, start by preparing sushi rice by washing short-grain Japanese rice until the water runs clear, then cooking it with water and rice vinegar. Once the rice is cooked, let it cool slightly before seasoning it with a mixture of sugar and salt. Next, prepare your fillings, which can include ingredients like raw fish, avocado, cucumber, or cooked shrimp. Lay a sheet of nori (seaweed) on a bamboo sushi mat, spread a thin layer of rice over it, leaving a small border around the edges. Add your desired fillings in a line along the bottom edge of the nori. Use the bamboo mat to tightly roll the sushi, applying gentle pressure as you roll. Once rolled, use a sharp knife to slice the sushi into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi on the side. Enjoy your homemade sushi!
